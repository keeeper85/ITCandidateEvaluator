<html><b>Find a substring</b><br>
The getPartOfString method must return the substring that begins with
the character after the first space and ends with the end of the word
that follows the 4th space.<br><br>
Example:<br>
<i>"CodeGym is the best place to learn Java."</i><br><br>

Result:<br>
<i>"is the best place"</i><br><br>

Example:<br>
<i>"Amigo and Diego are best friends!"</i><br><br>

Result:<br>
<i>"and Diego are best"</i><br><br>

If the input data is invalid, throw a StringTooShortException <br>
(create an exception).</html>
